## About the Collection

This collection consists of donations of small discarded objects collected between 2025 and 2027 from the bottoms of bags or in the pockets of donors. Items are primarily donated by community members in Pittsburgh, PA. Due to the nature of the collection process, most of the donors are friends and aquaintances of the creator, Reed Davidson. This donation/collection project is considered an art project as well as an archival project, and is hopefully going to be shown as an art instillation in a local Pittsburgh gallery space at the end of the ongoing collection process.
